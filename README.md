- # Everyday Actions Presentation

## 1. Look for - qidirib topmoq
   - *Example:* Nodir uyini qidirib topmoqchi.
   - *Translation:* Nodir is looking for his house.

## 2. Get up - uyg'onmoq
   - *Example:* Erkin ertalab barvaqt uyg'ondi.
   - *Translation:* Erkin got up early in the morning.

## 3. Wake up - uyg'oq
   - *Example:* Akbar barvaqt uyg'ondi.
   - *Translation:* Akbar woke up early.

## 4. Go out - chiqmoq (tashqariga)
   - *Example:* Do'stlar tashqariga chiqishga tayyorlanishdi.
   - *Translation:* Friends got ready to go out.

## 5. Come in - kirmoq
   - *Example:* Ziyovuddin tekshirish uchun kirib kelmoqchi.
   - *Translation:* Ziyovuddin is coming in to check.

## 6. Turn on - yoqmoq
   - *Example:* Ovqat pishirish uchun elektrni yoqing.
   - *Translation:* Turn on the electricity for cooking.

## 7. Turn off - o'chirmoq. 
   - *Example:* Mehmonlar chiroqlarni o‘chirib ketishdi.
   - *Translation:* The guests left, turning off the lights.

## 8. Put on - kiymoq
   - *Example:* Nargiza yangi libosini kiydi.
   - *Translation:* Nargiza put on her new dress.

## 9. Take off - yechmoq
   - *Example:* Kresloga o‘tirib tuflisini yechdi.
   - *Translation:* He took off his shoes sitting on the chair.

## 10. Sit down - o'tirmoq. 
    - *Example:* Sobir suhbatdan keyin o'tirdi.
    - *Translation:* Sobir sat down after speaking.
